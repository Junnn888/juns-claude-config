# LEARNINGS

Manual capture log. When you notice yourself correcting Claude on the same
thing repeatedly, add an entry here by hand. This deliberately is **not** an
automated reflector and **not** a skill — manual curation keeps it provenanced
and rot-free (the reasoning is in the design spec).

Periodically, promote stable entries into the global `CLAUDE.md` yourself.
Prune entries that have been promoted or proved irrelevant.

- **Lesson:** what Claude should do differently.
- **Trigger:** the situation/correction that prompted this.
- **Source:** where the rule comes from (your judgement / a doc / a bug).
- **Proposed CLAUDE.md delta:** the exact line you'd add, or "none yet — observing".
- **Status:** observing | promoted | dropped
